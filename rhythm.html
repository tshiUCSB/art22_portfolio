Ryhthm

A web-based rhythm game with note-mapping and produces a graphic visualization of player performance after finishing the song. Instead of visualizing just the sounds in a song, Ryhthm intends to include audience interaction as part of the visualization. With a graphic generated for each player with each playthrough, there is something more that remains after interacting with the song to share with others other than a numerical score and the individual experience that the player walks away with.

Press A, W, D, F, H, J, K, or L on the keyboard in correspondence with the section that the note is located in when the note reaches the edge of the circle to hit the note.
